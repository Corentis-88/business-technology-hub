## Cambridge National performance-table points

Current DfE accountability tables assign Level 2 Technical Award grades performance points (for example D* 8.5, D 7, M 5.5, P 4 in the referenced guidance). These are school accountability points, **not an exact statement that a Cambridge National grade equals a particular GCSE grade**. The student site should explain the Cambridge National grade scale directly and avoid misleading “equivalent to” labels.

## Facts requiring MEA confirmation

### Blocking for a fully accurate student planner

- Which cohort the initial site is for and whether the 2027 final dates are the correct active dates.
- Exact internal mock dates and progress-assessment windows.
- R068, R069, R094 and R095 internal start, supervised-assessment and hand-in deadlines.
- Whether students may be entered for both Business and Enterprise; if yes, school instructions for the 21 May 2027 clash.
- Which programming language and IDE are used for J277.
- Whether OCR Exam Reference Language is taught alongside the school language.
- Exact school/home access to Photoshop and any alternative iMedia software.
- Whether the October 2024 MEA curriculum maps remain current for 2026–27.
- Correct Year 11 Enterprise sequence, because the published map contains unrelated Pearson Business content after page 2.

### Governance and launch

- MEA/Prospere permission and brand rules for the logo.
- Public versus authenticated deployment.
- Subject specialists responsible for approving each course.
- Data protection, safeguarding and retention decisions for student accounts/progress.
- Licensing/embedding approval for third-party video and revision resources.
- Accessibility requirements and local SEND/access-arrangement signposting.

### Useful but non-blocking

- Preferred reading age/tone.
- House style for exam-answer scaffolds.
- Existing school subscriptions: Seneca, Smart Revise, KnowItAll Ninja, iMediaGenius, Pearson ActiveLearn or others.
- Links to MEA’s official exams, independent-study and wellbeing support.
- Whether teachers want original question authoring, assignment, class analytics and intervention lists in later phases.

## Update cadence

- Exam dates: when provisional and final timetables are published; final timetable always wins.
- Public papers/reports: after awarding-body release.
- Specifications/subject updates: termly automated check plus annual human review.
- Grade boundaries: after each relevant results series, clearly labelled historical.
- MEA maps/deadlines: at least once per academic year and whenever subject leads notify a change.
